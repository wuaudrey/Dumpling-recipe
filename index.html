Homemade Pork Dumplings

Ingredients

Filling:

1 pound ground pork
8 scallions (green onions)
1 tablespoon minced ginger
2 tablespoons sesame oil
1 tablespoon table salt

Wrapping:

2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup hot water

Sauce:

3 table spoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon rice vinegar
dash of sesame oil

Pan Frying:

2 tablespoons canola oil
2 tablespoons water

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together flour and salt. Then add water and mix with wooden spoon/spatula.
  2. With your hands, start forming the dough. It will be hard at the beginning but keep kneading dough for about 5-7 minutes. Form the dough into a ball.
  3. When ready, place dough into plastic bag or cover in plastic wrap and let it rest for 15 minutes on the kitchen counter.
  4. Divide the dough into 2 equal pieces. Put one piece back in the bag. Roll the other half into a sausage shape and cut it into even pieces.
  5. Flatten each piece with a hand and roll them out as thin as possible.
  6. Fill, fold, repeat!
